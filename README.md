# 🎬 60-Day YouTube AI Domination Plan (Faceless | 1 Hour/Day)

> From 0 to 1000+ Subscribers & ₹5K–₹25K in Revenue with AI Automation

## ⚙️ Strategy Overview

- **Channel Type**: Faceless YouTube using AI
- **Time Required**: 1 hour/day
- **Tools**: ChatGPT, ElevenLabs, CapCut, Pictory, Canva, Gumroad, TubeBuddy

---

## 📆 Phase 1: Build Foundation & Attack (Days 1–15)

**Goals:**
- Create your system
- Upload 5 Shorts + 2 Long AI Videos
- Setup Affiliate Links

| Day | Task |
|-----|------|
| 1 | Pick niche (Tech, Finance, Self-help, AI, Motivation) |
| 2 | Create YouTube channel, banner, description |
| 3 | Research 10 viral videos in niche |
| 4 | Script 3 videos using ChatGPT |
| 5 | Record voiceover with ElevenLabs |
| 6 | Create video using Pictory or CapCut |
| 7 | Upload with SEO-optimized title, tags, and thumbnail |
| 8–15 | Repeat: Script → AI Voice → Video → Upload |

✅ By Day 15:
- 5 Shorts
- 2 Long Videos
- Affiliate links in place

---

## ⚡ Phase 2: Grow Audience & Monetize (Days 16–40)

**Goals:**
- Consistent uploads (3–5 per week)
- Launch ₹99–₹299 Digital Product
- Capture emails

**Weekly Flow:**

| Day | Task |
|-----|------|
| Mon | Script 2 Shorts, 1 Long video |
| Tue | AI Voice + Visuals |
| Wed | Edit & Post Short |
| Thu | Script + Voice Long Video |
| Fri | Edit & Upload Long |
| Sat | Batch 2 Shorts |
| Sun | Analyze performance & adjust |

💰 Monetization:
- Add affiliate links (Amazon/tools)
- Sell mini product on Gumroad
- Mention products in videos

✅ By Day 40:
- 300+ Subs
- 10+ Shorts, 6+ Long Videos
- First sale (affiliate or product)
- Email form setup

---

## 🚀 Phase 3: Scale, Optimize, Dominate (Days 41–60)

**Goals:**
- Daily Shorts + 2 Longs/week
- Pitch for sponsorships
- Scale affiliate/product revenue

**Strategies:**
- Repurpose Shorts to TikTok & Reels
- Double down on high-performers
- Build Notion/Google Sheet system
- Reach out for collabs/sponsorships
- Launch 2nd product (Notion Template/Toolkit)

✅ By Day 60:
- 500–1000+ Subs
- 15+ Longs, 30+ Shorts
- ₹5K–₹25K Revenue
- Growing audience + email list
