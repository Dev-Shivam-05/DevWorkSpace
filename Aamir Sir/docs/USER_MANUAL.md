# User Manual - Aamir Shaikh Portfolio

## Introduction
Welcome to your new portfolio website! This guide will help you manage your site and understand its features.

## How to Manage Submissions
1. Navigate to `admin.html`.
2. Here you can see all the inquiries sent through the contact form.
3. Use the "Export All" button to get an Excel report of all leads.
4. Each user who submits the form will automatically download a PDF certificate of their inquiry.

## Updating Content
### 1. Projects
To add a new project, open `portfolio.html` and add a new `portfolio-item` div within the `portfolio-grid`. Ensure you set the correct `data-category`.

### 2. Blog Posts
To add a blog post, open `blog.html` and copy an existing `blog-card` structure. Update the text, date, and image.

### 3. Skills
To update your skill levels, open `index.html` and modify the `width` percentage in the progress bars under the Expertise section.

## SEO Tips
- Always use descriptive `alt` tags for images.
- Keep your meta descriptions updated in each HTML file's `<head>`.
- Regularly update your `sitemap.xml` if you add new pages.

## Support
For technical issues, refer to the `DOCUMENTATION.md` or contact your developer.
